Horse Life

lifestyle, animals, meaningful

10/01/2020

I am a horse, and I wanted to share a few thoughts with all of you. As a horse, I live my life in the great outdoors, running freely, and enjoying the simple pleasures of grazing in lush pastures. But there are some things that I have observed that I think are important for humans to understand. First and foremost, please remember that we are living beings with feelings and emotions. So please treat us with kindness and respect. Secondly, please remember that we are not just tools to be used for your entertainment or work. We deserve to be treated with dignity and to have our needs met. Lastly, please remember that we are not invincible. We can get sick, tired, or injured just like you. So please take care of us, provide us with proper nutrition, shelter, and medical care, and give us the love and attention that we crave. I hope that these thoughts have given you a glimpse into the life of a horse. Let's strive to live in harmony and respect each other's differences.

Ben Waghorn, Maeve Kelly and Eliza Gurney | KIT202 2024
